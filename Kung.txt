 The plot of the cartoon "Kung Fu Panda" tells the story of
 saving the Valley of Peace from the invincible master Tai Lung. 
 During the martial arts tournament, the unlucky and clumsy panda Po
 accidentally catches the master's eyes. The inhabitants of the valley
 by common decision choose him as the savior of the Dragon Warrior.
 According to the master, Pau is the very one who can be their savior.
 Boy Po will be trained with Tigra, Praying Mantis, Monkey, Viper and Crane.
 But the panda lacks confidence, he hardly believes that he can become a real warrior.
 But you need to believe in yourself to get an ancient scroll. Can the panda Po
 cope with all the difficulties?
